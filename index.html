ESTATE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

About EMS

The Estate Management System (EMS) is a robust, web-based platform developed by NIC Punjab for PSIEC to streamline and digitize all post-allotment services for Industrial Focal Points. Designed with a focus on convenience, transparency, and efficiency, EMS empowers allottees with a comprehensive suite of online services that are accessible anytime, from anywhere. Through EMS, allottees can seamlessly apply for various post-allotment services, manage all their PSIEC plots from a unified dashboard, monitor dues, receive real-time notifications, and make secure online payments. The platform also facilitates e-payment of water and sewerage bills and provides live tracking of service request statuses. Additionally, EMS enables PSIEC to issue official communications and updates directly to allottees, ensuring smooth and timely interactions. Whether managing a single plot or multiple properties, EMS brings simplicity, control, and digital transformation to industrial estate management across Punjab.

E-AUCTION

All interested bidders must register and enroll through the official portal: www.pbindustries.gov.in.

Bidders are strongly advised to ensure a stable high-speed internet connection while participating in the e-auction. PSIEC shall not be held responsible for any disruptions or connectivity issues on the bidder's end.

Any corrigendum, updates, or announcements related to the listed properties — including e-auction extensions — will be published exclusively on www.psiec.punjab.gov.in. Bidders are advised to check the website regularly for the latest information.

Corner plots are indicated by the symbol (P) in the property listing table. Please note that an additional 10% charge will be applicable for corner plots.

Complete details regarding available plots — including sizes, dimensions, layout plans, reserve prices, as well as the e-auction help manual, eligibility criteria, Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) requirements, and full terms & conditions — can be accessed at www.psiec.punjab.gov.in
